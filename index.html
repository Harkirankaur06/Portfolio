Hey, I’m Harkiran

I build thoughtful, visually intentional digital experiences where engineering meets creativity.

I’m a third-year Computer Science student pursuing a B.Tech with Honors in Cloud Computing at Amity University, Noida. I enjoy working on projects that sit at the intersection of engineering and experience — where functionality is strong, but the interface still feels thoughtful and human.

Over time, I’ve explored web, mobile, backend, AI, and interactive systems, building everything from AI-powered health assistants and sustainability tools to gamified awareness platforms and visually rich digital experiences. I’m especially drawn to projects where technology is used to explain, simplify, or encourage meaningful engagement.

Beyond development, I’ve been actively involved in leadership and co-curricular spaces. As a Class Representative, I work closely with peers and faculty, and I regularly participate in hackathons, technical clubs, and cultural initiatives — often representing my university at national-level events. These experiences have shaped how I communicate, collaborate, and take ownership of what I build.

Right now, I’m focused on creating interactive, well-crafted products — experiences that feel intentional, intuitive, and slightly magical. I care about details, flow, and polish, and I’m constantly learning how to make technology not just work, but *feel right*.
